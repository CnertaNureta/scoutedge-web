Group A presents a clear pecking order: Morocco (FIFA #14) and Ecuador (FIFA #31) are the favorites, with Canada (FIFA #49) and South Africa (FIFA #59) also ahead in the rankings. ## Squad Analysis

Uzbekistan's squad is anchored by players from the Uzbek Super League, supplemented by professionals in Russia, Turkey, and the Gulf leagues. The team's tactical identity is compact and disciplined, sitting deep and striking on the break with pace and precision.

### Strengths

Set-piece delivery is a significant weapon. Uzbekistan's dead-ball routines have produced critical goals throughout qualification. The midfield is technically proficient, with players comfortable on the ball and capable of picking passes through defensive lines. Goalkeeping is strong, with tournament-experienced options.
