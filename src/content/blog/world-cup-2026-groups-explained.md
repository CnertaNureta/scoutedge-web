## FAQ

### How many groups are in World Cup 2026?

There are 12 groups at the 2026 FIFA World Cup, each containing 4 teams. This is an increase from the 8 groups of 4 used in previous World Cup tournaments, reflecting the expansion to 48 teams.

### Which is the Group of Death at World Cup 2026?

Group C (Morocco, Brazil, Scotland, Haiti) is widely considered the Group of Death at World Cup 2026. It features 2022 semi-finalists Morocco and five-time champions Brazil, meaning at least one elite team will face early elimination.

### How many teams qualify from each World Cup 2026 group?

The top 2 teams from each group qualify automatically for the knockout rounds. Additionally, the 8 best third-placed teams across all 12 groups also advance. This means 32 out of 48 teams reach the Round of 32.

### When is the World Cup 2026 group stage?

The World Cup 2026 group stage runs from June 11 to June 26, 2026. Each team plays 3 matches during this period, for a total of 72 group-stage fixtures across 16 host cities.

### Can a team still qualify for the knockouts if they finish third in their group?

Yes. World Cup 2026 introduces a third-place qualification route. The 8 best third-placed teams (out of 12 groups) advance to the Round of 32 alongside the top 2 from each group, giving 32 total teams in the knockout bracket.
