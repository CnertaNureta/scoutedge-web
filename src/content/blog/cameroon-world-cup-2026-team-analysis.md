# Cameroon World Cup 2026 Preview: The Indomitable Lions Seek Another Giant-Killing Run

Cameroon are World Cup royalty in African football. From Roger Milla's celebrations in 1990 to the shock of beating Argentina in their opening match of the same tournament, the Indomitable Lions carry a legacy of fearless, explosive performances on football's biggest stage. Ranked 48th in the FIFA standings with an Elo rating of 1,616, Cameroon arrive in 2026 with a squad that blends Premier League quality with African flair.

## Group B Outlook

Portugal (FIFA #6) loom large at the top of Group B, but the battle between Cameroon, Iran (FIFA #20), and Bolivia (FIFA #82) for second place is wide open. Cameroon's physicality and attacking talent make them the most dangerous of the three challengers, but Iran's organization poses a specific tactical problem.

## Squad Strength

The defense, anchored by **Christopher Wooh** (Rennes) and **Enzo Ebosse** (Udinese), has improved but remains the area of greatest concern.
