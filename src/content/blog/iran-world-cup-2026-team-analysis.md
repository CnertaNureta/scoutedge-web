# Iran World Cup 2026 Preview: Team Melli's European Stars Target Knockout Stage

Iran have become one of the most consistent Asian football nations at World Cup level, and the 2026 tournament offers perhaps their best chance yet to reach the knockout rounds. Ranked 20th in the FIFA standings with an Elo rating of 1,691, Team Melli possess a squad that blends European top-flight experience with the organization and discipline that has defined Iranian football.

## Group B Outlook

Drawing Portugal (FIFA #6), Cameroon (FIFA #48), and Bolivia (FIFA #82) gives Iran a clear target: finish second. Portugal are the obvious group favorites, but Iran's record against African opposition at World Cups is strong, and Bolivia's altitude-dependent style will not translate to North American venues.

## Squad Strength

The spine of this team plays in Europe's top leagues. **Mehdi Taremi** (Inter Milan) is a world-class striker whose hold-up play, aerial ability, and finishing are elite. **Sardar Azmoun** (Roma) provides pace and movement alongside or behind Taremi. **Alireza Jahanbakhsh** (Feyenoord) adds experience and creativity from wide areas.

The midfield is industrious with **Sadegh Moharrami** (Dinamo Zagreb) providing overlapping runs from right-back, and **Saeid Ezatolahi** offering midfield protection. **Alireza Beiranvand** in goal has proven himself at the highest level.
