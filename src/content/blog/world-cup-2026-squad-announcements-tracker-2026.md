As of May 11: Argentina, Spain, France (preliminary), Germany, England, Portugal (preliminary), Brazil, Belgium, Italy, Morocco, Japan, South Korea, Colombia, Croatia, Senegal, Australia, and others. Full status in tracker above.

**How does KickOracle's Chemistry Index change when squads are confirmed?**
