## The Narrative

Group A is Mexico's Group. Played primarily on home soil (with the exception of the Los Angeles opener), with Mexican diaspora filling US stadiums and Azteca for the decisive games, this is the tournament Mexico have been building toward since 1970. The Round of 16 curse — seven consecutive exits at that stage — is the psychological weight every Mexican carries into June. Group A is where it begins to lift, or confirms itself once more.

For South Korea, this is Son Heung-min's last World Cup in all likelihood. His first-round exit in Qatar, where he was nursing a fractured eye socket, robbed football of the performance he owed. Group A gives him the opportunity for redemption.
