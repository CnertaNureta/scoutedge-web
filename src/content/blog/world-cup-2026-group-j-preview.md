# World Cup 2026 Group J: Argentina's to Lose — But Can Algeria Surprise?

The defending champions enter one of the tournament's more navigable groups — but Algeria's high-press system and tactical improvement over the last two years make the opening game more meaningful than it appears on paper. Whether Argentina cruise through or stumble in the opener will set the tone for their entire tournament.

## The Narrative

Group J is Argentina's group — but the tournament begins for them here, in Atlanta, against an Algeria side with nothing to lose and a pressing system designed to create chaos in the first 20 minutes. If Messi is rotated for the opener, Argentina are more vulnerable than the 68% figure implies.
